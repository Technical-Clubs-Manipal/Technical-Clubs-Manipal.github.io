Manipal Information Security Team (MIST)

"We are a team of Information and Network Security enthusiasts who have gathered knowledge about the field through workshops, online courses, books and research. We now want to spread the knowledge we have gained to other students with an interest in this ever-growing area of computer science. We want to ensure that students approach this field the right way and to provide them with a platform to enhance and practice their skills in the same. Last but not the least, we are not hackers. So if you are here with the intention of hacking into your ex's facebook account, I am afraid this is not the place for you."

International Society of Automation - Manipal

The International Society of Automation is an organisation of engineers, technicians, consultants and corporate trainers specializing in the fields of instrumentation, control and automation engineering. We are the bridge between the students and the professional world of engineering. Using the resources at hand, we open the door to workshops, seminars, industrial visits and internships.

ASME Manipal Chapter

American Society of Mechanical Engineers is a professional association which in its own words, promotes the art, science and practice of multidisciplinary engineering and allied sciences around the globe. ASME's Manipal chapter aims to explore each domain of engineering and not just limit ourselves to just one. We have had several events,workshops and talks, none of which were based on a single domain. Rather they were ech time based on something new, something unexplored

Formula Manipal

Formula Manipal, a student engineering project based in Manipal, Karnataka comprises of a group of passionate students of Manipal Academy of Higher Education who aim to design, conceptualize, fabricate, test and race single seater, open-wheel Formula Style Combustion and Electric race cars. Formula Manipal starting as an idea in 2007, has come up with 8 extremely intriguing cars till date which have competed nationally and internationally in various platforms including Italy, UK, Austria, Germany and Czech Republic.
