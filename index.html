ACM Manipal

ACM Manipal is the official student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) for Manipal University. ACM is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society. ACM ICPC (also referred to as the “Olympics of Programming Competitions”), and the A.M. Turing Awards, are two of ACM’s most prominent events.

ACM Manipal, is very different from any other technical club on campus because we have a flat hierarchy in place. That means that our Core Committee consists of students from all years across all branches. We believe that age cannot be considered a factor to determine one’s competency.

Website: https://manipal.acm.org

: acm.manipal@gmail.com

Formula Manipal

Formula Manipal, a student engineering project based in Manipal, Karnataka comprises of a group of passionate students of Manipal Academy of Higher Education who aim to design, conceptualize, fabricate, test and race single seater, open-wheel Formula Style Combustion and Electric race cars.

Formula Manipal starting as an idea in 2007, has come up with 8 extremely intriguing cars till date which have competed nationally and internationally in various platforms including Italy, UK, Austria, Germany and Czech Republic.

Website: https://www.formulamanipal.in

thrustMIT

thrustMIT started off in early 2016 as a small group of rocket enthusiasts with the aim of promoting High-Power-Rocketry in India. The Team intends to take part in the Spaceport America Cup, organized by Experimental Sounding Rocket Association and held in the United States every June. Earlier known as Intercollegiate Rocket Engineering Competition, it is the biggest platform in the world for UG and PG students to showcase their Rocket Engineering skills.

With a large participation from the best universities throughout the world and extensive media coverage, it is a huge event thousands of students, researchers and businessmen look forward to. thrustMIT also aspires to spread knowledge, promote, foster and bring about technical innovations in the ingenious field of rocketry. The team has come a long way since its inception, and is constantly working towards perfection.

Website: www.thrustmit.in

: team.thrustmit@manipal.edu
