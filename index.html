Formula Manipal

Formula Manipal, a student engineering project based in Manipal, Karnataka comprises of a group of passionate students of Manipal Academy of Higher Education who aim to design, conceptualize, fabricate, test and race single seater, open-wheel Formula Style Combustion and Electric race cars.

Formula Manipal starting as an idea in 2007, has come up with 8 extremely intriguing cars till date which have competed nationally and internationally in various platforms including Italy, UK, Austria, Germany and Czech Republic.

ACM Manipal

ACM Manipal is the official student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) for MAHE. ACM is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society. ACM ICPC (also referred to as the “Olympics of Programming Competitions”), and the A.M. Turing Awards, are two of ACM’s most prominent events.

ACM Manipal, is very different from any other technical club on campus because we have a flat hierarchy in place. That means that our Core Committee consists of students from all years across all branches. We believe that age cannot be considered a factor to determine one’s competency.

Website: https://manipal.acm.org

: acm.manipal@gmail.com

IECSE Manipal

Established in 2007, IECSE is the official club of the Department of CSE. Aimed to promote technical awareness amongst students, IECSE provides a platform for students to hone their technical skills. One of the largest technical clubs in Manipal, it is a community of tech enthusiasts. The club organizes weekly workshops for programming, designing & development and is known to hold regular contests for coders & nerds alike. The annual fest Prometheus brings all the geeky minds together in a variety of competitions.

IECSE boasts of huge international participation in its bi-annual scavenger hunt HawkEye/CyberHawk. Other events include PSUC/DS/OOP Workshops held to guide the students with their academics and Placement Talks. The club members have proved their mettle regularly in Microsoft's Code.Fun.Do., GSoC, AngelHack, ICPC and numerous other campus/national events.
