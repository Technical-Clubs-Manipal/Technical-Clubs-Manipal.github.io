Institute of Engineers for Biotechnology, Manipal Chapter (IEBt)

IEbt is a premier society of Indian Engineers from MIT, Manipal, which organizes technical and non-technical activities relating to different aspects of biotechnology. Symbiot is a national level symposium conducted annually by IEBt, Manipal with the aim of providing participants an exposure to a real life work environment relating to various workshops. It includes various paper and poster presentations, along with talks from eminent people from the industry. Every year more than 200 students from all over the country take part in the event.
